## 📄 BẢN NHÁP ABSTRACT 1 TRANG (Deadline 12/04)

### AegisFlow AI: AI-powered GIS Digital Twin for Urban Flood Resilience

**Track:** Smart City — Disaster Management & Urban Sustainability

**Problem Statement**
Southeast Asian cities face rapid urbanization and severe climate change impacts, with urban flooding costing billions annually and endangering lives. Current disaster response systems are **reactive** — authorities respond AFTER flooding occurs. Da Nang, Vietnam, with its coastal geography and heavy seasonal monsoons, exemplifies this massive challenge across ASEAN metropolitan areas.

**Proposed Solution**
AegisFlow AI is an advanced Digital Twin platform integrating **GIS** and **AI** that **predicts and proactively manages** urban flooding. The system models the city's network as a dynamic graph and uses machine learning and real-time sensor data to forecast flood levels and safe evacuation routes.

**Key Capabilities**
1. **AI Prediction Engine** — Deep learning models analyze weather APIs, topography, and historical flood data to predict high-risk flood zones.
2. **Dynamic Safe Routing** — AI routing automatically removes heavily flooded nodes to compute the safest evacuation routes.
3. **Real-time Flood Radar** — Crowdsourced data and IoT sensors update flood coordinates dynamically onto a live map.
4. **Vulnerability Assessment** — AI calculates Vulnerability Scores to prioritize rescue efforts for high-risk demographics.

**Technical Architecture**
- AI Engine: Python, Amazon Bedrock
- Backend API: Node.js (Express), PostgreSQL/PostGIS
- Frontend: Next.js (React), Leaflet.js
- Data Pipeline: WebSockets for real-time live data streaming

**Impact & Feasibility**
- **Immediate:** Rapidly guides citizens away from dangerous "flood traps".
- **Scalable:** Deployable to any ASEAN city facing monsoon or tidal flooding issues.
- **Aligned with SDGs:** SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 13 (Climate Action).
